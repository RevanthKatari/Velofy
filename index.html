About Velofy Speed Speedometer

Velofy Speed is a powerful and accurate GPS speedometer application that helps you track your speed in real-time. Whether you're driving, cycling, running, or walking, our app provides precise speed measurements using your device's GPS capabilities.

Key Features

  • Real-time GPS speed tracking with high accuracy
  • Toggle between km/h and mph units instantly
  • Beautiful, minimalist interface with dynamic color themes
  • Works offline - no internet connection required
  • Progressive Web App - install on any device
  • Battery efficient GPS usage
  • No ads, completely free to use

How It Works

Velofy Speed uses your device's GPS sensor to calculate your current speed. The app continuously monitors your location changes and computes your velocity with high precision. The speed display updates in real-time, giving you instant feedback about your movement speed.

Perfect For

  • Drivers wanting to monitor their vehicle speed
  • Cyclists tracking their riding speed
  • Runners and joggers measuring their pace
  • Anyone curious about their movement speed
